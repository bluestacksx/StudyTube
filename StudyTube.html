About StudyTube

StudyTube is a distraction-free YouTube client designed specifically for students who want to focus on educational content.

Features

  • Block shorts, feed, and related videos
  • Educational content filter
  • Focus mode with password protection
  • Custom playlists and subscriptions
  • Data saver mode
  • Works offline (PWA)
  • No tracking, no ads, no account needed

Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect any personal information, track your activity, or show ads. Your privacy is our priority.
